Different types of theft leave different recognizable patterns — like diseases leaving different symptoms.

We call these patterns Loss Fingerprints.

01 ⚡

PHASE BYPASS

A wire tapped before the meter — only 2 of 3 phases measured.

Consumption drops sharply to ~66% of normal usage and stays constant. This indicates one phase is bypassed in a three-phase connection.

The AI detects this sudden step-change and flags it within days.

84% ACCURACY
02 🔧

METER TAMPER

Smooth unnatural decline over time.

Instead of sudden drops, usage declines smoothly over time. This is unnatural — real usage always fluctuates.

The AI detects low-variance patterns that indicate manipulation.

76%ACCURACY
03 🔒

BILLING FREEZE

Identical readings across cycles.

Meter readings remain constant across multiple billing cycles. Zero usage is statistically impossible for active connections.

This is one of the highest-confidence theft signals.

97%ACCURACY
04 🌙

GHOST LOAD

Night-only heavy usage.

High electricity usage occurs only during late-night hours. Often linked to unauthorized industrial usage.

The AI compares day vs night consumption to detect anomalies.

86%ACCURACY
05 📉

SLOW BLEED

Hidden small continuous loss.

Small but consistent under-reporting of usage. Hard to detect at individual level.

AI compares with similar households to find long-term deviations.

70%ACCURACY
